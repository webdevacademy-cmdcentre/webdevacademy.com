About the HTML & CSS Course

Welcome to the WebDev Academy's HTML & CSS Course. Our mission is to provide top-notch education in web development, focusing on the foundational languages of the web: HTML and CSS. This course is designed to equip you with the skills necessary to create stunning, responsive websites that look great on any device.

In this course, you'll learn:

  • The basics of HTML, including how to structure your web pages using elements and attributes.
  • How to use CSS to style your web pages, including layouts, colors, fonts, and more.
  • Responsive design principles to ensure your websites work well on mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.
  • Best practices for web development to create clean, maintainable code.

Upon completion of the course, you'll receive a certificate of achievement. This certificate can be a valuable addition to your resume, showcasing your skills to potential employers and helping you stand out in the competitive field of web development. Many of our students have used this certificate to advance their careers, secure promotions, or start new jobs in the tech industry.
